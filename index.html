Master Thesis:
Fast convolutional deep learning for image segmentation

My master thesis, written at DTU wit Ole Winther and Anders Lindbo Larsen as supervisors. In the thesis I used Deeppy and Cudarray to implement a fast image segmentation technique using DNN and applied it to ISBI Challenge: Segmentation of neuronal structures in EM stacks.

Deeppy and Cudarray
